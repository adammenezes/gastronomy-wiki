## Overview
Stocks, Sauces, and Soups represent fundamental pillars of [[culinary arts]], forming the backbone of [[flavor]] and [[texture]] in countless [[dishes]] across diverse [[cuisines]]. This [[category]] encompasses the foundational [[liquids]] ([[stocks]]), the flavorful accompaniments ([[sauces]]), and the comforting, often complete, [[meals]] ([[soups]]). Mastery of these [[areas]] is essential for [[developing]] a comprehensive [[understanding]] of [[cooking techniques]] and [[flavor building]], particularly in [[Classical French Cuisine]] where they originated as core [[components]].
